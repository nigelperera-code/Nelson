What helps an introduction land well

Not a data room — just enough for a useful first conversation.

  • Last 2 years accounts (or management numbers if available)
  • Customer mix + any contracts (high level is fine)
  • Team structure and owner dependency
  • Why now / what the owner wants next
  • Any compliance / accreditation notes (e.g., UKAS-related)
