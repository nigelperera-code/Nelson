How we behave

  • Discreet: controlled conversations, no unnecessary involvement
  • Clear: we give fast “yes / no / not yet” feedback
  • Calm: simple process, minimal disruption to the owner
  • Fair: sensible structure and respectful negotiation
  • Reliable: we do what we say we’ll do

What works best for introductions

  • A short blind profile is perfect to begin
  • High-level financials (even approximate) help
  • We’re happy to speak owner-to-owner quickly if appropriate
  • If it’s not for us, we’ll say so early

We aim to be easy to work with — and to protect your relationship with the client.

What we look for
Established, dependable service businesses.

We’re open-minded on sector. We’re more interested in businesses that have repeat or recurring demand, strong reputations, and teams that can keep things running as owners step back.

  • Repeat or recurring revenue
  • Predictable demand
  • Good people and a steady team
  • Sensible, sustainable profitability
  • Owners thinking about succession
The process
Simple, controlled, and respectful.
  • Short introductory call
  • Light review of headline numbers
  • Meet the owner (private and calm)
  • Agree next steps, timing, and structure
“We’re not here to create theatre. We’re here to be straightforward.” — Nelson Lord
Preferred way to send an opportunity

A short email with the basics is ideal. A blind profile is fine to start.

Include (if available)

  • Location / geography
  • What the business does (1–2 lines)
  • Approx revenue and profit
  • Reason for sale and timing
  • Anything sensitive we should know
