Confidentiality & process

A sensible process that protects what you’ve built.

Owners often want the same things: no rumours, no disruption, no months of theatre. We agree. This page explains how we keep things discreet and straightforward.

Owner-first
Focused diligence
Continuity-led handover

Confidentiality is a discipline.

We keep the circle small. We don’t broadcast opportunities. We avoid creating noise inside a business.

In practice

  • We use an NDA when sharing sensitive information
  • We don’t contact staff, customers or suppliers without agreement
  • We avoid asking for unnecessary documents early
  • We plan communications carefully, when timing is right

How we behave

  • We don’t “shop” opportunities around
  • We don’t request heavy diligence until there’s genuine fit
  • We don’t retrade for sport
  • If it’s not for us, we say so quickly
  • We keep disruption away from your team

How we think about value

Fair value + sensible structure + certainty. Often the best outcome is achieved through structure — not noise.

First call (15 minutes)

Owner-only. Not an interrogation. Just fit, priorities, and whether it’s worth a second conversation.
