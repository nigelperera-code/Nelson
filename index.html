What we buy
We’re open-minded on sector.

What matters is the shape of the business: repeat or recurring revenue, predictable demand, a reputation built over time, and a team that can keep things running as an owner steps back.

If your business depends on constant marketing, rapid growth, or one key individual holding everything together, we’re probably not the right home — and we’ll say so honestly.

After a sale
In most cases, not much changes.

  • Look after long-standing customers
  • Support managers so the business doesn’t rely on one person
  • Add structure and visibility where it genuinely helps
