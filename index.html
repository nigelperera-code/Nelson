Our thesis: compliance-led services compound well

In many sectors, regulation and standards create repeatable demand, and customers value reliability and evidence, not just price.

Compliance drives recurring work

Where duty of care and documentation matter, customers value consistency. That creates stickier relationships and more predictable planning.

Clear rules create clarity

Our current focus areas sit within well-defined regulatory frameworks. We see this as a strength: clear rules create clarity, consistency, and long-term demand for businesses that operate properly.
