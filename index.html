What we look for

We are open-minded on sector. What matters is the shape of the business. Repeat or recurring revenue, predictable demand, a reputation built over time, and a team that can keep things running as an owner steps back.

We are usually not the right home for businesses that depend on constant marketing, rapid growth, or one key person holding everything together. If that is the case, we will say so honestly and promptly.

After a transition

In most cases, not much changes. We focus on continuity and steady support.

  • Keep the team in place
  • Look after long-standing customers
  • Support managers so the business does not rely on one person
  • Add structure and visibility only where it genuinely helps
