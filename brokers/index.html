Who we are

We build platforms, not portfolios. We are not flippers or trend-chasers. We buy cashflow that survives the owner. :contentReference[oaicite:3]{index=3}

What we focus on

  • Predictable recurring revenue
  • Regulatory or structural demand drivers
  • Replaceable founders
  • Strong cash conversion
  • Fragmented markets

We use two filters to reduce subjectivity and avoid wasted time. :contentReference[oaicite:4]{index=4}

Filter 1
REALS (first call)

Structural durability test applied during initial conversations. No spreadsheet needed. :contentReference[oaicite:5]{index=5}

  • R — Recurring / regulated revenue
  • E — Essential service (holds up in downturns)
  • A — Asset-light
  • L — Low concentration (avoid binary risk)
  • S — Systems-based (revenue stays if founder leaves)
Filter 2
CASHFLOW (after numbers)

Financial validation filter after reviewing financials. Validates sustainability and replaceability. :contentReference[oaicite:6]{index=6}

  • C — Contracted or predictable revenue
  • A — Attractive gross margins (target 40%+ ideally)
  • S — Short debtor cycles (30–60 days preferred)
  • H — High EBITDA-to-cash conversion
  • F — Founder replaceable
  • L — Low fixed overhead
  • O — Operational simplicity
  • W — Working capital discipline
Target profile (summary)

Financial

  • £150k–£500k sustainable EBITDA
  • 40%+ gross margin (ideally)
  • <10% revenue per customer
  • Cash conversion within ~60 days

Operational

  • Recurring contracts / repeat model
  • Systems-driven operations
  • Scalable via bolt-ons
  • Not dependent on founder heroics

We prefer vendor finance where appropriate, clear governance, and no active legal/regulatory issues. :contentReference[oaicite:7]{index=7}

Broker fast check

Please confirm these 6 points before sending. :contentReference[oaicite:8]{index=8}

  • Sustainable EBITDA ≥ £150k?
  • Revenue ≥ 50% recurring or repeat?
  • Founder willing to stay for transition?
  • No single customer >30% of revenue?
  • Not dependent on heavy paid ads?
  • Clear reason for sale?
Quick rank (1–5)

Score instinctively. Average ≥ 3.5 → send to us. :contentReference[oaicite:9]{index=9}

  • Revenue predictability
  • Owner replaceability
  • Market defensibility
  • Succession motivation
  • Scalability
The 15-minute rule

If we can’t answer these confidently in 15 minutes, the opportunity isn’t ready. :contentReference[oaicite:10]{index=10}

  1. Is revenue predictable?
  2. Does profit survive the owner?
  3. Why do customers stay?
  4. What could break the business?
  5. Why is the owner selling?
