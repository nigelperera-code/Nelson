Confidentiality
We keep things private. Always.
We understand how sensitive these conversations can be — for you, your staff, your customers, and your family. We treat confidentiality as non-negotiable.
Request a confidential call 07309 690 039

Our commitments

  • We don’t contact staff, customers, suppliers, or landlords without your approval
  • We keep discussions private and controlled
  • We involve third parties only when necessary and on a need-to-know basis
  • We don’t circulate opportunities or talk about them casually
  • If we’re not the right fit, we’ll say so early

What you can expect from the process

  • A short, calm first call
  • Only reasonable information requests
  • Clear next steps (or a clear stop)
  • No unnecessary disruption to day-to-day operations

You stay in control of the pace and who knows what, and when.

A simple promise
“Nothing leaves the room without your agreement.” — Nelson Lord

How we keep things controlled

  • We keep communication to a small, agreed group
  • We share information carefully and only when needed
  • We avoid contacting anyone connected to the business until you approve it
  • We keep the process calm and proportionate

If you want extra reassurance

  • We are happy to sign an NDA early
  • We can agree a simple “who knows” list
  • We can agree a communication plan for any sensitive steps
